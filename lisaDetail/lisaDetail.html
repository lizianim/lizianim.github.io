Lisa Ziebermayr

Currently studying @ Design Art Technology ArtEZ, Arnhem.

With a formal background in Graphic Design, I decided to expand my artistic practice to a deeper focus in human / technology interaction and artistic research through a multi-media approach.

In my practice I investigate technology and material, and work with their artifacts to create a narrative with and about their systems.

In real life, I stare at the sky and like to find interesting corners in my peripheral vision (when I'm not watching the stars).
