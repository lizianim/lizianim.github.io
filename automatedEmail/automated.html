This Email is automated. [2023]

play-by-email game

As a new employee at "Sparecorp Incorporated Inc." the player is tasked with navigating a confusing network of automated email customs in the office.

"This Email is automated." uses Outlook to deconstruct the inner workings of automated emails. The story builds a speculative reality in which the main communication happens between email servers instead of people.
