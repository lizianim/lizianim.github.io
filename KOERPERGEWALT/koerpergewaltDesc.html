KÖRPERGEWALT [2023]

hypertext fiction

Returning is often an understanding. The world around us, what we are, what we are looking for. What happens when there is no way back or forward, if we are caught in an infinite loop of overthinking?

The work explores the themes of regret and blame through a hypertext narrative, in which the basic functions of the site are restricted to one action only: trying to refresh the page.
